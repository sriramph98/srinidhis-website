## üìã Detailed Guide

### 1. üè† Hero Section (Homepage Top)

**Location:** `Hero` document ‚Üí `Profile` tab

**Image Field:** Profile Photo

**Purpose:** Your professional headshot displayed at the top of the homepage

**Specs:**
- Displayed as a circle
- Recommended: Square image (1:1 ratio)
- Minimum: 400x400px
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Shows your professional image

**How to Add:**
1. Go to **Hero** in Sanity Studio
2. Click on the **Profile** tab
3. Upload your photo to **Profile Photo**
4. Use hotspot to adjust focal point if needed

### 2. üìÑ Section Content

**Location:** `Section` documents ‚Üí `Media` tab

**Image Field:** Section Images

**Purpose:** Visual content specific to each section type

#### Section-Specific Image Usage:

##### LinkedIn Optimization Section
- Upload images showing LinkedIn profile examples
- Before/after optimization results
- Profile screenshots
- Engagement metrics

##### Resume Writing Section
- **‚≠ê Image Carousel** - These images appear in the carousel!
- Upload multiple resume examples
- Template samples
- Before/after resume comparisons
- Add 3-6 images for best carousel effect

##### Coaching & Job Search Section
- Coaching session photos
- Career guidance illustrations
- Success story visuals
- Meeting/consultation images

##### Why Me Section
- Credentials and certifications
- Achievement highlights
- Personal branding images
- Awards or recognition

##### How It Works Section
- Process diagrams
- Step-by-step workflow illustrations
- Infographics
- Visual guides

##### Writing Services Section
- Writing sample screenshots
- Document examples
- Service illustrations
- Portfolio pieces

**How to Add:**
1. Go to **Sections** in Sanity Studio
2. Select the section you want to edit
3. Click on the **Media** tab
4. Click **Add item** under Section Images
5. Upload your image
6. Fill in **Alt Text** (required for accessibility)
7. Optionally add a **Caption**

**Image Specs:**
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended width: 1200-1800px
- Aspect ratio: Varies by section (carousel images work best at 16:9 or 4:3)
- Optimize images before upload (use tools like TinyPNG)

### 3. üí¨ Testimonials

**Location:** Individual `Testimonial` documents ‚Üí `Author Info` tab

**Image Field:** Client Photo

**Purpose:** Professional photo of the person giving the testimonial

**Specs:**
- Displayed as a circle
- Optional (can be left empty)
- Recommended: Square image (1:1 ratio)
- Minimum: 200x200px
- Format: JPG or PNG

**How to Add:**
1. Go to **Testimonials** in Sanity Studio
2. Select or create a testimonial
3. Click on the **Author Info** tab
4. Upload photo to **Client Photo** (optional)
5. If no photo is provided, a placeholder initial will show

## üé® Image Best Practices

### General Guidelines

1. **File Size**
   - Keep images under 2MB each
   - Use compression tools before uploading
   - Website automatically optimizes, but smaller = faster

2. **Dimensions**
   - Profile photos: 400x400px minimum
   - Section images: 1200-1800px width
   - Testimonial photos: 200x200px minimum

3. **Format**
   - JPG for photos
   - PNG for graphics with transparency
   - WebP for best compression (if available)

4. **Alt Text**
   - Always provide descriptive alt text
   - Helps with accessibility (screen readers)
   - Improves SEO
   - Example: "Professional resume template with modern design"

### Image Optimization Tools

- **TinyPNG** - https://tinypng.com
- **Squoosh** - https://squoosh.app
- **ImageOptim** - https://imageoptim.com (Mac)
- **JPEG Optimizer** - Built into most image editors

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Q: Which section images appear in the carousel?
**A:** Only images in the **Resume Writing** section appear in the carousel on the website.

### Q: What if I don't have a testimonial photo?
**A:** That's fine! Leave it empty, and the website will show the client's initial in a circle instead.

### Q: How many images should I add to a section?
**A:** 
- Resume carousel: 3-6 images work best
- Other sections: 1-3 images typically sufficient
- More is okay, but consider page load times

### Q: Can I reorder images?
**A:** Yes! In the Section Images array, drag and drop to reorder. First image = first in carousel.

### Q: Do I need images for every section?
**A:** No, images are optional for most sections. Add them where they enhance your content.
